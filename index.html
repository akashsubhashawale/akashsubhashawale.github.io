About

.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Jammu. My work focuses on sustainable machining and grinding, advanced abrasive finishing, and characterization of surface integrity using non-destructive techniques.

  • Objective: Explore and share knowledge in industry and academia, and contribute through research in biomedical implants and sustainable manufacturing.
  • Research themes: eco-friendly lubri-cooling (MQL/Cryo-MQL), ultrasonic vibration-assisted processes, surface texturing, and post-processing of additively manufactured components.

Research interests

.

Sustainable grinding (MQL / Cryogenic) Advanced abrasive machining Ultrasonic vibration–assisted machining Surface texturing Difficult-to-cut materials Surface integrity Magnetic Barkhausen noise (NDT) Laser cladding Post-processing of AM components

Research

Focus areas and approach

My research explores sustainable manufacturing strategies that improve surface integrity, enhance tribological performance, and reduce environmental impact.

  • Optimization of grinding/machining environments (dry, MQL, Cryo-MQL, hybrid approaches).
  • Ultrasonic vibration–assisted machining and grinding for improved process stability.
  • Surface integrity assessment using magnetic emission / Barkhausen noise analysis.

If you collaborate with me, typical outcomes include:

  • Improved surface finish and reduced thermal damage.
  • Eco-friendly process parameter optimization using data-driven approaches.
  • Experimental + characterization workflow (SEM/XRD/MBN and related tools).
